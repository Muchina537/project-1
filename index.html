Basic Rules of Investing


by Kenn Muchina
Investing
I write about business and investing

World best investor and CEO,Berkshire Hathway,Warren Buffet has remained an icon of excellence when it come to investing.He attributes his success to his mentor Benjamin Graham(1894-1976).

Here are the three basics rules of ivesting:

1.Don't loose Money

Invest in platforms that offer 100% principal protection.

2.Diversify your Portfolio

An ideal ratio would be 50% in common stock with 50% in bonds.This will protect you against inflation or your capital loosing value.

3.Manage Your Risks

It is practically impossible to outwin all investing risks whether in stocks,mutual fund or bonds.You should therefore aim at distributing your portfolio wisely

You better investwisely if your dream is joining the 1% popullation that runs the world.The good thing is that is quite easy to invest only if you follow the three basic rules of investing.
